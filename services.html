Services End‑to‑end support for songs & rights

We work with writers, producers, artists, labels and visual media partners. Below is a modular overview you can adapt as your Notion “Services” page evolves.

Publishing

Writer signings & catalog management

We sign and represent songwriters, producers and artists, handling registrations, royalty administration and long‑term catalog strategy.

  • Work registration with CMOs and PROs in Korea, Europe, UK, US and beyond.
  • Monitoring and chasing missing royalties across territories.
  • Split negotiation and documentation for sessions and co‑writes.
  • Audit‑ready catalog tracking via Notion / internal databases.
Sync & Creative

Music for film, TV, games & brands

We connect songs and composers with moving images – from Korean streaming platforms to international campaigns.

  • Brief‑based pitching for Korean dramas, films and OTT content.
  • Custom composition and score supervision with our roster.
  • Licensing and clearance (master / publishing) in cooperation with labels.
  • Usage reporting and royalty follow‑up per territory.
A&R & Sessions

Song camps, remote sessions & cuts

We develop songs with our writers for K‑pop, J‑pop, C‑pop and global artists, working both in‑person and remotely.

  • Song camp curation in Seoul and abroad.
  • Topline / track pitching to labels, management and A&Rs.
  • Song feedback and pre‑production support.
  • Admin support for demos, splits, and file delivery.
Consulting & Partnerships

Asia strategy, sub‑publishing & affiliates

For international rights‑holders looking at Asia, KYTE supports entry strategies, local partnerships and catalog rollout.

  • Sub‑publishing and affiliate models in Korea, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Macao, Vietnam and Thailand.
  • Local registrations, translations and metadata support.
  • On‑ground representation at showcases, festivals and markets.
  • Custom reporting and royalty reconciliation per region.
