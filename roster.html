Roster Writers · Artists · Producers

Charlie McClean

Producer · Songwriter
Highlights
  • IVE – “WOW”
  • Diana Ross – “I Still Believe”
  • kiiikiii – “GROUNDWORK”

Charlie McClean is a Grammy‑nominated, multi‑platinum producer and songwriter based in London. Her work spans K‑pop, soul, pop and alternative music, with credits for IVE, Diana Ross, LÉON, Sam Ryder, Dragonette and Icona Pop. Collaborations with Canadian artist Aysanabee earned her two Juno Awards, solidifying her as a sought‑after collaborator in both mainstream and indie spaces. Beyond the studio, her music has appeared in film, TV and campaigns for platforms and brands including Disney+, Apple, Rick Owens, Lidl and Mercedes‑Benz.

International · UK

Sojeso

Guitarist · Producer · Music Director
Highlights
  • RM – “HEAVEN”
  • Simon Dominic – “TTFU”
  • Giriboy – “Hurejasik (Prod. by SOJESO) (feat. Woo)”

Sojeso is a guitarist and producer whose work moves fluently between K‑pop, advertising, fashion and contemporary dance. He co‑wrote and performed on BTS RM’s single “Heaven” and has served as music director for productions including Cartier Magical Night (2024), We Had No Choice But to Move (2025, Seoul Arts Center) and FIVEVIBE (2025, National Theater of Korea). He has produced music for campaigns by COS, GUCCI, COMME des GARÇONS, Samsung x Margiela and contributed to teasers for NCT DREAM, Taeyeon, Seulgi, Jimin, RIIZE, Baekhyun and more. He is also guitarist of indie band The Godat and active in multiple collaborative projects.

Seoul · Multi‑disciplinary

Goodmorning Pancake

Producer · Songwriter · Topliner
Highlights
  • Goodmorning Pancake – “Getup”
  • DPR IAN – “Ballroom Extravaganza”
  • DPR LIVE – “Yellow Cab”

Goodmorning Pancake is a singer‑songwriter duo consisting of producer ampoff and vocalist Sunny Shin. They are known for delicate R&B‑leaning productions with emotional depth, sharp storytelling and refined arrangements. Their single “Getup” gained mainstream recognition as the theme for Channel A’s variety show Heart Pairing, while producer ampoff has contributed to DPR IAN and DPR LIVE, bridging K‑pop, indie and alternative scenes. They oversee the full creative process from songwriting to production direction, building a warm yet distinct sonic world.

Galaxy Express

Artist · Songwriter · Topliner
Highlights
  • Galaxy Express – “Youth Without Youth”
  • NewJeans – “I FEEL COKE”

Galaxy Express is a three‑piece rock band representing the garage rock, punk and psychedelic side of Seoul’s underground. Known for explosive live shows and a bold stage presence, they channel the rebellious energy of early New York punk and 1960s Korean psychedelia. Over almost two decades they have become one of Korea’s veteran indie bands, winning at the Korean Music Awards and performing at SXSW, Canadian Music Week, Focus Wales, Liverpool Sound City, Summer Sonic and more.

Yangbans

Artist · Songwriter · Producer
Highlights
  • Yangbans – “Through the Tunnel”
  • Epik High – “STRAWBERRY”
  • Ahn Ye Eun – “Octopus' Dream”

Yangbans are a six‑piece band from Seoul whose music celebrates the harmony of nature, self‑described as “Wind & Flow” or Pungnyu rather than conventional rock or R&B. Drawing on the cultural and literary sensibilities of the historical yangban class, they infuse their compositions with a distinctly Korean artistry. Since forming in 2013 under frontman Jeon Beom‑seon, they have remained a consistent presence in the Korean music scene. Their 2016 release “Revolution” won Best Rock Song at the Korean Music Awards, cementing their role in shaping a modern Korean narrative.

Seoul · Wind & Flow

Kim Kate

Producer · Songwriter
Highlights
  • Audio branding for Rexona 2024
  • Club & fashion‑driven releases across Grime, heavy thumper and warehouse techno

Heewon Oh, known as Kim Kate, is a sound designer, music producer and computer music educator, formerly based in London and now working out of Seoul. Ranging across grime, heavy thumper and warehouse techno, his productions are known for bold yet intelligent sound design. His music has been featured on BBC Radio 1, Rinse FM, Worldwide FM and Seoul Community Radio. He has taught Music Computing at Goldsmiths, University of London, and has collaborated with fashion brands including Louis Vuitton and thisisneverthat, highlighting his multidisciplinary reach.

Lee DeBong

Producer · Songwriter
Highlights
  • Lang Lee – There is A Wolf
  • Yeoyu & Seolbin – COMEDY

Debong Lee is a Seoul‑based producer, performer and artist who emerged as one of the most prominent figures in the Korean indie scene in 2024. Known for deep collaboration with artists, he crafts tailored arrangements that highlight each collaborator’s unique texture, leading to constant demand from fellow songwriters. He produced Lang Lee’s There is A Wolf, a landmark work in Korean indie folk, and COMEDY by Yeoyu & Seolbin, both of which received dual awards at the Korean Music Awards in their respective years.

Seoul · Indie / Folk

Kim Gaon (KreixKim)

Producer · Songwriter
Highlights
  • Kreixkim – Father's Buffalo
  • Raw by Peppers – “3”

Better known as KreixKim, Gaon Kim is a South Korean electronic music producer and techno DJ whose work often incorporates indigenous sounds and field recordings from Korean landscapes. His music leans toward shamanistic, dark industrial aesthetics. He began as frontman of Raw by Peppers, a Seoul‑based psychedelic rock and shoegaze band, and released his debut solo EP Father's Buffalo in 2021, inspired by an intense two‑year stay in Berlin.

Hyunansang (Hyun AhnSang)

Producer · Songwriter · Topliner
Highlights
  • Eddie and the Bricks – “Pause”
  • Hyunansang – Puppy

Hyunansang is a singer‑songwriter and producer known for delicate emotional sensibility and a wide musical spectrum. With roots in bands such as Crooks, Letters and Eddie and the Bricks, he has developed a distinctive solo voice. His track “Pause” reached a wider audience after appearing in the hit TV series Transit Love 3 and later on MBC’s Good Day. His 2019 EP Puppy showcases his ability to seamlessly weave narrative and sound. Actively writing, composing, performing and arranging, he continues to expand his universe across solo and band projects.

Kim Hankyeol (Kim HanGyeol)

Producer · Songwriter · Topliner
Highlights
  • Kim Hankyeol – “Perfume” (Netflix The Trunk OST)
  • TILDE – “Dark Night”

Hangyeol Kim is a singer‑songwriter and composer known for an intimate emotional palette and carefully crafted sound. Starting in the band TILDE, he has expanded his own artistic universe through introspective and evocative songs. “Perfume”, which he composed and performed for the Netflix series The Trunk, brought him to broader public attention, adding subtle yet powerful emotional tension to the show. Go Dam

Producer · Songwriter
Highlights
  • Go Dam – Alternate Dimensions
  • Go Dam – Promised Dimension

Go Dam is the electronic music persona of Seoul‑based producer and composer Taewon Kim. Drawing on a long lineage of electronic music, he creates dynamic and detailed soundscapes with strong structure and sonic depth. His debut 12" EP Alternate Dimensions (2017) established him on the global underground scene, followed by Promised Dimension (2019), which earned acclaim within the braindance and electro communities. His track “Force of Will”, released on Kalahari Oyster Cult’s compilation The Chants of The Holy Oyster with S.O.N.S, further cemented his reputation. His releases have been curated by respected record stores like Hard Wax and Bleep.

Wildberry

Producer · Songwriter
Highlights
  • Wildberry – “Castaway”

Wildberry is a South Korean singer‑songwriter and producer who blends classical piano training with experimental sound design and refined emotional storytelling. Her work explores the cosmos, nature, inner emotion and love, moving fluidly through R&B, electronic and soul. Her 2024 debut album AGAPE marked a new chapter, centered on unconditional love and presented with strong visual identity. Beautiful Disco

Producer · Songwriter
Highlights
  • CHAEYOUNG (TWICE) – “BF”
  • Crush & LEE HI – “Tip Toe”
  • Beautiful Disco – “Quarto”

Based in Seoul, hip‑hop producer Beautiful Disco is known for warm analog textures, sampling and minimalist production style, making him a key figure in the lo‑fi hip‑hop scene. He has released projects such as Soothing Beats for Savage Snakes and cross‑border collaborations including “Namsan” with Berlin‑based producer Saib and “Jeju” with Oslo‑based Kristoffer Eikrem. In the mainstream, he has contributed to K‑pop with “BF” for CHAEYOUNG (TWICE), “Tip Toe” for Crush & LEE HI, and “Good Morning” for DAHEE, combining his signature sound with contemporary pop sensibility.

Danpyunsun

Artist · Songwriter · Producer
Highlights
  • Danpyunsun & The Moments Ensemble – Hail to the Music (2024)

Danpyunsun is a Korean musician, producer and composer celebrated for poetic lyrics and avant‑garde songwriting. Blending jazz, folk and art rock, his work explores emotional extremes – from sublimity and absurdity to pleasure and melancholy – through intricate arrangements and vivid storytelling. In 2024 he founded Danpyunsun & The Moments Ensemble; their debut album Hail to the Music won Album of the Year and Best Modern Rock Album at the 2025 Korean Music Awards, a rare feat for an independent project. He is widely regarded as one of Korea’s most distinctive voices, bridging literary, emotional and experimental approaches.

Korea · Art Rock / Experimental
